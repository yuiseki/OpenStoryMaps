## What is this?

OpenStoryMaps is a software designed to tell a story along with maps.

Very many people want to tell their story, with maps.

They also need to tell their story with data visualization.

OpenStoryMaps allows you to tell a story with maps and data by simply writing Markdown.

This web page demonstrates what is possible with OpenStoryMaps.
